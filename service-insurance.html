Personal Insurance & Wealth Protection

The right insurance strategy is about more than ticking a box. It’s about protecting the people and lifestyle that rely on you if the unexpected happens. At Greystone Wealth Capital, we help you structure Life, Total & Permanent Disability (TPD), Trauma and Income Protection cover so that your debts, income, business and family are safeguarded in a tax-effective and sustainable way.

We review your existing cover (inside and outside super), identify gaps and overlaps, compare leading insurers, and design a strategy that balances quality definitions, affordability and long-term suitability. You’ll understand exactly what you’re covered for, how premiums are funded, and how your policy integrates with your broader wealth and retirement plans.

How We Structure Your Cover

Our advice is tailored to your situation, not driven by a single product or provider. We consider ownership, tax, cash flow and claims outcomes to ensure your cover works when you need it most.

  • Detailed needs analysis for Life, TPD, Trauma and Income Protection based on debts, income, dependants and goals.
  • Comparison of existing policies and new options, including cover held inside and outside super.
  • Advice on policy ownership (personal, superannuation, SMSF, business), funding structure and tax implications.
  • Consideration of stepped vs level premiums and long-term affordability.
  • Clear Statement of Advice outlining recommended cover, insurer, benefits and key definitions in plain English.
  • Ongoing review service to keep your cover aligned with life changes such as children, promotions, new loans or business changes.

  • How much cover do I actually need?

    We calculate cover based on your existing debts, ongoing family living expenses, children’s education, business obligations and your spouse’s needs. The goal is to avoid both underinsurance and paying for cover you don’t need.

  • Should my insurance be inside or outside super?

    It depends on your cash flow, tax position and the type of cover. Some benefits of holding cover in super include cash flow relief and tax concessions on contributions; however, there can be trade-offs in definitions, beneficiary control and claim tax. We step you through the pros and cons and recommend an appropriate mix.

  • What is the underwriting and claims support process?

    We help prepare and submit your applications with full medical and financial disclosure to reduce surprises at claim time. If a claim arises, we work alongside you and the insurer to help navigate requirements and support you through the process.
