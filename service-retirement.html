Strategic Retirement Planning

Retirement planning isn’t just about reaching a number—it’s about ensuring your wealth provides the lifestyle, freedom and security you’ve worked for. At Greystone Wealth Capital, we develop retirement strategies that align with your goals, taking into account superannuation, investments, tax, and income streams.

Whether you’re five years away from retirement or already transitioning, we’ll guide you through contribution strategies, pension structures, and government benefits to help maximise your after-tax income and preserve your capital over the long term.

Our Approach to Retirement Planning

Our process focuses on aligning your money with your life goals — ensuring your super, investments and income streams work together efficiently for a comfortable and confident retirement.

  • Superannuation optimisation and contribution strategies (salary sacrifice, concessional and non-concessional).
  • Transition to Retirement (TTR) and Account-Based Pension setup for tax-effective income.
  • Cash flow and budget analysis to ensure your income meets your lifestyle needs.
  • Investment allocation to balance income stability, growth and capital preservation.
  • Centrelink and Age Pension strategies to enhance your retirement income.
  • Estate planning and beneficiary structuring to protect your wealth for future generations.

  • How much do I need to retire comfortably?

    The amount depends on your lifestyle goals, living costs, and whether you are eligible for government support. We model different scenarios to determine how much super and investments you’ll need to achieve your preferred standard of living.

  • When should I start transitioning to retirement?

    Many Australians begin preparing 5–10 years before retirement. This allows time to build contributions, adjust investments, and establish income streams such as Transition to Retirement (TTR) pensions to reduce tax and smooth the change from work to retirement.

  • How do I manage tax in retirement?

    Super pensions in the retirement phase are generally tax-free for those over 60, but the structure of your investments, timing of withdrawals and use of other assets can affect your overall tax efficiency. We help design a tax-effective withdrawal strategy to maximise your income.
