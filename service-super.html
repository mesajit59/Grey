Superannuation Review & Strategy

Superannuation is one of the most powerful wealth-building tools available to Australians. Yet many people hold multiple accounts, pay unnecessary fees, or invest in options that don’t align with their goals. Our advisers conduct a full review of your superannuation to ensure your fund, investments and insurance align with your objectives and risk profile.

We compare your existing funds with suitable alternatives, review insurance held within super, and identify ways to improve long-term performance. You’ll receive a clear, documented strategy outlining recommendations and projected outcomes — giving you full control and confidence over your retirement savings.

What’s Included in Our Advice

Our holistic process ensures every aspect of your super works together — not just your investments but also your contributions, tax efficiency, and insurance coverage.

  • Comprehensive review of your super fund performance, fees and insurance options.
  • Consolidation of multiple or inactive super accounts to reduce unnecessary fees.
  • Review of your investment allocation based on your risk profile and time horizon.
  • Recommendations for contribution strategies to boost retirement savings.
  • Review of insurance held within super to ensure cost-efficiency and adequate protection.
  • Clear Statement of Advice outlining actionable steps, rationale and projected outcomes.

  • How often should I review my superannuation?

    We recommend reviewing your superannuation at least once a year or when major life changes occur — such as a new job, salary change, property purchase or starting a family — to ensure your strategy remains aligned with your goals.

  • Can you review my existing insurance held inside super?

    Yes. We review the quality, cost and relevance of your insurance held within super to determine if it aligns with your needs. If improvements can be made, we provide clear recommendations.

  • Do you assist with fund consolidation and rollovers?

    Absolutely. We manage the entire process of consolidating super funds, ensuring your benefits and insurance are protected and that rollovers are completed efficiently and securely.
